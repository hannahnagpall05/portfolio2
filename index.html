About

Where I come from, what I’m doing now, and where I want to go next.

I’m Hannah, an Honors Computer Science student and former NCAA Division I tennis player. I began my CS journey at Alabama A&M University and now continue it at Texas A&M University–Kingsville.

I work with Java, Python, C++, SQL, HTML, and CSS, and I’m drawn to problems where rigorous thinking and real-world impact meet—whether that’s backend engineering, AI-in-retail externships, or Raman spectroscopy research.

My long-term goal is to pursue a Master’s degree at a top research university, contributing to high-quality engineering work while staying grounded in the habits I built on court.

How I approach work

  • Discipline – Years of training and competition taught me to stick with hard things.
  • Consistency – Whether it’s early practices or debugging, I show up and do the reps.
  • Pressure-ready – Matches and deadlines both demand composure and clear decisions.
  • Ambition – I’m not chasing “busy”; I’m chasing meaningful, high-standard work.
